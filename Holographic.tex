\begin{abstract}
The Holographic Principle suggests that the description of a volume of space can be thought of as encoded on a lower-dimensional boundary to the region. This paper introduces the \textbf{Holo-Net}, a neural architecture that demonstrates the Anti-de Sitter/Conformal Field Theory (AdS/CFT) correspondence using modern attention mechanisms.
